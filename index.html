about me.

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Kaelynn Amodia, and I am a game developer and recent graduate from Rochester Institute of Technology with a degree in Game Design & Development. I have a passion for creating immersive experiences through game design, web development, UI/UX, and interactive storytelling.

Growing up, I was always drawn into the worlds of video games — from button-mashing my way through Tekken 5 or spending hours trying to 100% NieR:Automata. The feeling of being completely immersed in a fictional world has always stuck with me. Now, I strive to create those experiences for others — to make games and experiences that spark imagination, tell meaningful stories, and truly come alive.
