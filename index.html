about me.

Hello! I am delighted to meet you! My name is Kaelynn Amodia, and I am pursuing a degree in Game Design & Development at Rochester Institute of Technology. Growing up, my interests and hobbies have always flourished; fashion, art, music, dancing, coding, writing-- each one a small piece of who I am today. But one activity stood out from the rest. From mashing buttons on my controller playing Tekken 5, to spending countless hours trying to 100% NieR:Automata, I had grown to love the boundless creativity and immersive worlds that video games had to offer.

I aspire to combine my newfound skills in coding and technology with the boundless depths of my imagination to create games that immerse players into worlds beyond their wildest dreams. I want to recreate the fascination I experienced as a child, to transport others into worlds where imagination reigns supreme. This is what drives me forward on this exciting journey of game creation.
