Looking for Group ("LFG")

about this project.

Looking for Group is an upcoming website designed to connect developers and designers for collaborative, project-based work. Its goal is to create a safe, low-pressure environment where users can grow their skills while forming meaningful connections with others.

Unlike traditional networking sites like LinkedIn and Handshake, which tend to focus on job hunting and professionalism, LFG takes on a more casual and community-oriented approach. Whether you are starting a project or just want to meet other creatives, LFG is designed to be a welcoming space that encourages creativity and growth.

As part of the UI/UX development team, my team and I focused on shaping the aesthetics and user experience of the site. I worked primarily in Figma, collaborating closely with my team to create wireframes and high-fidelity mockups that reflected the site's goal.

During my first semester on the project, I also served as team lead for the “Virtual Space” feature (now called "Studios"). Our team conceptualized and prototyped an interactive game-like environment where users could create personal avatars and design their own "Studio" — similar to games like Club Penguin or Habbo Hotel. Although this feature was later put on hold to prioritize the networking aspect, the process still taught me a lot about brainstorming, planning, and team leadership.
