PIN-BRAWL

about this project.

PIN-BRAWL is an arcade-style multiplayer platform fighting game developed in Unity by a team of 5. Inspired by games like Duck Game and Knight Club combined with the pinball/outrun aesthetic, we wanted to create an experience that was casual and fun, in which the game is easy to pick up and play but difficult to master.

You can play with up to 4 players, and each player can choose from 4 different weapons and colors. Players have four lives, in which they battle to the death in each round until one person is left standing. Attacking is directional, and players can use the grappling feature to launch themselves at other players and travel around the maps.

I worked on UI/UX development as well as produced some of the pixel art environment assets and animations.
